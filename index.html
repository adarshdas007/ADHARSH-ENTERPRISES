Our Story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

At Adharsh Enterprise Sofa Works, we bring over 15 years of dedicated expertise in sofa repair, upholstery, and furniture restoration to every home and office in Bengaluru. Founded on the principles of quality craftsmanship and genuine customer care, we have earned the trust of hundreds of families, apartment communities, cafes, and corporate offices across the city.

We believe every piece of furniture tells a story — and our skilled artisans work with precision and passion to restore that story, using premium-grade fabrics, high-density foam, and time-tested techniques. From a worn-out cushion to a complete fabric overhaul, we treat every project as our finest work.

With timely pickup and delivery, transparent pricing, and a 4.88-star Google reputation, we don't just repair sofas — we renew comfort and bring fresh life to your living spaces.
